# 🌟 Even More Visual Features!

I have further enhanced the **Learning Mode** to be even more engaging and visual.

## 📺 New Features

### 1. 🎥 Video Integration
- **What's New**: The app now searches for and displays **recommended educational videos** (YouTube, etc.) related to the topic.
- **How it works**: Below the lesson, you'll see a row of video cards with thumbnails and durations. Clicking them opens the video in your browser.
- **Benefit**: Kids can watch a quick video to reinforce what they just read.

### 2. 🎨 Beautiful "Fun Fact" Cards
- **What's New**: "Fun Facts" are no longer just text. They are now displayed in **colorful, distinct cards** with a green border and background.
- **How it works**: The app automatically detects the "Fun Fact" section in the AI's response and wraps it in a special styled box.

### 3. 💅 Rich Text Styling
- **What's New**: The entire lesson is now beautifully styled:
    - **Headings**: Colorful and underlined.
    - **Blockquotes**: Orange borders for important info.
    - **Code/Keywords**: Highlighted in pink/grey.
    - **Tables**: Clean, zebra-striped tables for data.

## 🚀 How to Test

1. **Restart the App**:
   ```bash
   ./START_APP.command
   ```
2. **Go to Learning Mode**:
   - Login -> Learning Tab.
3. **Ask a Topic**:
   - Type: **"Dinosaurs"** or **"Space"**.
4. **See the Magic**:
   - **Images**: Top gallery.
   - **Text**: Streaming with beautiful colors and cards.
   - **Videos**: Scroll down to see recommended videos!

Enjoy the ultimate visual learning experience! 🚀
